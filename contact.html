When should I visit Tanzania?

Tanzania is a year-round travel destination. The best time for a safari is during the dry season (June–October), when wildlife viewing is at its best. High season is July–March, and low season is April–May.

Do I need a visa to visit Tanzania? +
Yes, most travelers need a visa. Check your country’s specific requirements beforehand.
How do I obtain a visa for Tanzania? +
Apply online via the Tanzania eVisa portal or obtain one on arrival at major entry points.
What kind of animals can I expect to see on a safari? +
Expect to see lions, elephants, giraffes, leopards, wildebeests, zebras, and more.
What should I pack for a safari in Tanzania? +
Bring neutral clothing, a hat, sunscreen, binoculars, bug repellent, and a camera.
Any health precautions before going on safari? +
Consult your doctor about vaccines and malaria meds. Bring hand sanitizer and first-aid items.
What is the currency in Tanzania? +
The currency is the Tanzanian Shilling (TZS). USD is also widely accepted in tourist areas.
What’s included with Precious Moments Safaris? +
Accommodations, meals, guide, game drives, and park entry fees are typically included.
What’s NOT included in the safari package? +
International airfare, tips, travel insurance, drinks, and personal expenses are excluded.
How do I book a safari with you? +
You can book directly via our website, email, or call our travel experts for assistance.
