Why Visit Zanzibar Beach Holiday

Zanzibar is an island located in Tanzania. It is famous for its stunning tropical beaches circled with palms, the historical Stone Town, and the beautiful spice plantations. For beach lovers, it offers world class hotels, diverse water sports, snorkeling with astonishing marine life at Mnemba Atoll, and for those who prefer adventure. Similarly, for honeymooners it is a delight. Zanzibar is indeed a captivating island.

FAQs – ABOUT ZANZIBAR

What is the best time to visit Zanzibar?

The best time is during the dry seasons from June to October and December to February, offering perfect beach weather and calm seas.

Do I need a visa to travel to Zanzibar?

Yes, most travelers need a tourist visa to enter Tanzania, which includes Zanzibar. It can be obtained online or on arrival.

What currency is used in Zanzibar?

The Tanzanian Shilling (TZS) is the official currency, though US dollars are widely accepted in hotels and tourist areas.

Is Zanzibar safe for tourists?

Zanzibar is generally safe, especially in tourist zones. However, travelers should take standard precautions, like avoiding isolated areas at night.

What activities can I do in Zanzibar?

Enjoy snorkeling, scuba diving, spice tours, cultural visits to Stone Town, dhow cruises, and beach relaxation.

WHAT TO DO

Top Activities in Zanzibar Beach

Mnemba Island

Explore a protected marine reserve with vibrant marine life such as lionfish, stingrays, and sea turtles. Swim, snorkel, and enjoy a chance to swim with the dolphins.

Dolphin Watching at Kizimkazi

Head to Kizimkazi on the southern coast of the island to watch and swim with wild dolphins. This is a must-do activity for nature lovers.

Stone Town Tour

Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Site: Wander through the narrow, winding streets of Stone Town. Visit historical sites like the House of Wonders, Sultan’s Palace, Old Fort, and Slave Market.

Prison Island (Changuu Island)

A short boat ride from Stone Town, visit Prison Island, home to giant tortoises, and explore the historic prison that once held slaves.

Jozani Forest Tour

Visit the Jozani Chwaka Bay National Park to explore Zanzibar’s native wildlife, including the endangered red colobus monkeys.

Visit the Spice Farms

Take a tour through spice plantations to see how cloves, nutmeg, cinnamon, and other spices grow, and enjoy tasting them fresh.

Sunset Cruise

Take a traditional dhow boat for a sunset cruise. You'll sail around the coastline of Zanzibar and witness a breathtaking sunset.

Safari Blue Tour

A day trip on a traditional dhow that includes snorkeling, swimming with turtles, enjoying a seafood BBQ on a sandbank, and exploring small islands.

Zanzibar Butterfly Centre

Visit the Zanzibar Butterfly Centre to learn about the conservation of butterflies and enjoy walking through the lush gardens.

Explore the Rock Restaurant

Enjoy a meal at the famous Rock Restaurant, located on a rock in the Indian Ocean, just off the coast of Bwejuu.

Cultural Experiences

Explore Zanzibar’s unique blend of African, Arab, Indian, and European cultures. Visit local markets, try traditional Zanzibari food like Ugali, Zanzibar pizza, and pilau rice.

Maluum Cave

Overview: Swim with turtles in a natural cave while learning about sea turtle conservation. A great way to support their preservation and enjoy a serene environment.

Zanzibar Sub Marine Tour

You can experience an exciting and unique underwater adventure through a submarine tour! The Zanzibar Submarine is one of the few places in the world where you can take a real submarine ride to explore the ocean’s depths without needing to be a diver.
