9-Day Camping Safari Package in Tanzania

9-Day Camping Safari Package in Tanzania

Tour Overview

This 9-day camping safari is crafted for adventurers who want to immerse themselves in Tanzania’s stunning landscapes and rich wildlife while staying in well-organized campsites. The itinerary covers the most iconic wildlife parks in Tanzania, where you’ll witness the Big Five, experience the Great Migration in the Serengeti, and explore the remarkable Ngorongoro Crater. Each night, you’ll rest under the African sky in comfortable tents, surrounded by the sounds of nature. This itinerary offers a perfect blend of adventure, wildlife, and the classic safari camping experience. Trip Style Camping Safari | Private Safari | Game Drives | Nature & Wildlife | Budget-Friendly

Day to Day Trip

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha – Tarangire National Park

Your camping adventure begins upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, where your guide will meet you. After a short briefing, you will depart for Tarangire National Park. Morning Departure: Enjoy a scenic 2.5-hour drive from Arusha to Tarangire National Park. Along the way, take in the rural landscapes and glimpse the majestic baobab trees that dot the horizon. Mid-Morning Game Drive: Begin your safari adventure with a game drive through Tarangire, known for its large herds of elephants and picturesque landscapes. Your guide will introduce you to the wildlife and tell stories about the park’s ecosystem. Picnic Lunch in the Park: Enjoy a picnic lunch surrounded by the stunning scenery of Tarangire. Afternoon Game Drive: Continue exploring Tarangire in the afternoon, with opportunities to spot giraffes, zebras, lions, and even leopards.
• Camp Setup: In the evening, arrive at your public campsite where your crew will set up tents and prepare a hearty dinner around the campfire.
Meals Included: Lunch and Dinner
Accommodation: Tarangire Campsite
Key Activities:
• Game drives in Tarangire National Park
• Scenic picnic lunch
• Evening campfire
Wildlife: Elephants, lions, giraffes, zebras, wildebeests, and leopards..

Day 2: Tarangire to Serengeti National Park

After breakfast, you will set out for the world-famous Serengeti National Park, where you will begin to experience the beauty of Tanzania’s endless plains. Morning Departure: After an early breakfast at your campsite, depart for Serengeti National Park. The drive takes you through the scenic Ngorongoro Conservation Area, offering beautiful views of the highlands. Lunch Stop at Ngorongoro Crater Rim: Have a picnic lunch at a viewpoint overlooking the Ngorongoro Crater. Afternoon Game Drive in Serengeti: Upon arriving in the Seronera region of the Serengeti, embark on an exciting game drive. This area is known for its rich wildlife, including large predator populations such as lions and leopards. Campsite Setup: In the evening, arrive at your campsite in Serengeti. Settle into your tent while your camp crew prepares dinner under the stars.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation: Seronera Public Campsite
Key Activities:
• Scenic drive through the Ngorongoro Conservation Area
• Afternoon game drive in Serengeti National Park
• Camp setup in the Serengeti
Wildlife: Lions, leopards, cheetahs, elephants, buffaloes, and zebras.

Day 3: Full-Day Safari in Serengeti National Park

Day 3 is dedicated to a full day of exploration in the Serengeti, where you’ll experience the Great Migration and other extraordinary wildlife moments. Sunrise Game Drive: Start your day early with a sunrise game drive, where you might spot predators like lions and cheetahs hunting in the cool morning air. Bush Breakfast: After your game drive, enjoy a bush breakfast surrounded by the sights and sounds of the Serengeti. Mid-Morning and Afternoon Game Drives: Continue your exploration, focusing on areas where the Great Migration is taking place (seasonal). You’ll have the chance to witness thousands of wildebeests and zebras as they traverse the Serengeti plains in search of fresh grazing grounds. Return to Camp: After a full day of game drives, return to your campsite for a relaxing evening around the campfire, enjoying dinner under the stars.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation: Seronera Public Campsite
Key Activities:
• Full-day game drives in Serengeti National Park
• Bush breakfast in the wilderness
Wildlife: Wildebeests, zebras, lions, leopards, cheetahs, elephants, and buffaloes.

Day 4: Serengeti to Northern Serengeti (Kogatende)

On Day 4, you will travel to Northern Serengeti, known for its dramatic Mara River crossings during the Great Migration. Morning Game Drive: Enjoy a morning game drive in the central Serengeti before departing for Kogatende. Scenic Drive to Northern Serengeti: After your morning drive, head to Northern Serengeti, where the Mara River crossings take place. This is one of the most thrilling moments in the migration as wildebeests and zebras attempt to cross the crocodile-infested river. Campsite Setup in Kogatende: In the evening, arrive at your campsite in Northern Serengeti. Relax around the campfire while your crew prepares dinner.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation: Kogatende Public Campsite
Key Activities:
• Morning game drive in Serengeti
• Scenic drive to Northern Serengeti
• Campsite setup in Kogatende
Wildlife: Wildebeests, zebras, crocodiles, lions, and elephants.

Day 5: Full-Day Safari in Northern Serengeti (Mara River)

Day 5 is focused on exploring the Mara River area, where the most dramatic wildlife events of the migration take place. Morning Game Drive: Start the day with a game drive along the Mara River, where you might witness one of nature’s greatest spectacles as wildebeests and zebras cross the river. Picnic Lunch: Enjoy a picnic lunch by the riverbank, giving you uninterrupted access to wildlife viewing. Afternoon Game Drive: In the afternoon, continue exploring the northern Serengeti’s rolling hills and river valleys, where predators like lions and leopards are frequently seen hunting. Return to Camp: After an exciting day, return to your campsite for a relaxing evening with dinner and storytelling around the campfire.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation: Kogatende Public Campsite
Key Activities:
• Full-day game drives in Northern Serengeti
• Mara River crossing viewing
Wildlife: Wildebeests, zebras, crocodiles, lions, leopards, and cheetahs.

Day 6: Northern Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater

On Day 6, you’ll leave the Serengeti and travel to the famous Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the best places to spot the Big Five. Morning Game Drive: Enjoy a final game drive in Northern Serengeti before heading towards Ngorongoro. Scenic Drive to Ngorongoro Crater: After your morning game drive, embark on a scenic drive to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, where you’ll descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for a game drive. Afternoon Game Drive in Ngorongoro Crater: The Ngorongoro Crater offers some of the best wildlife viewing in Tanzania, with opportunities to see lions, elephants, and the rare black rhino. Camp Setup: After your crater tour, arrive at your public campsite on the crater rim for dinner and an overnight stay.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation: Simba Campsite
Key Activities:
• Morning game drive in Northern Serengeti
• Afternoon game drive in Ngorongoro Crater
Wildlife: Black rhinos, lions, elephants, buffaloes, zebras, and flamingos.

Day 7: Full-Day Safari in Ngorongoro Crater

Today, you will enjoy a full day exploring the Ngorongoro Crater, one of the most unique safari destinations in the world. Morning Game Drive: Start your day with an early morning descent into the crater for a game drive, where you’ll have excellent chances to see the Big Five. Luxury Picnic Lunch: Enjoy a picnic lunch by a hippo pool, surrounded by the crater’s rich biodiversity. Afternoon Game Drive: After lunch, continue exploring the crater’s diverse habitats, from open grasslands to forests, where you can see elephants, zebras, and buffaloes. Return to Camp: In the late afternoon, return to your campsite for dinner and an evening around the campfire.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation: Simba Campsite
Key Activities:
• Full-day game drives in Ngorongoro Crater
• Picnic lunch by the hippo pool
Wildlife: Lions, black rhinos, elephants, buffaloes, zebras, and flamingos.

Day 8:Ngorongoro Crater to Lake Manyara National Park

On Day 8, you’ll travel to the beautiful Lake Manyara National Park, known for its tree-climbing lions and flamingos. Morning Departure to Lake Manyara: After breakfast, depart for Lake Manyara National Park, located along the Great Rift Valley escarpment. Morning Game Drive in Lake Manyara: Upon arrival, enjoy a game drive along the lake’s shores, where you can spot flamingos, hippos, and large herds of elephants. Picnic Lunch: Have a picnic lunch inside the park, taking in the views of the Rift Valley. Afternoon Game Drive: Continue your exploration of Lake Manyara in the afternoon, where you may spot tree-climbing lions and a variety of bird species. Campsite Setup: In the evening, arrive at your campsite near Lake Manyara for dinner and overnight camping.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation: Lake Manyara Public Campsite
Key Activities:
• Morning and afternoon game drives in Lake Manyara National Park
• Scenic picnic lunch
Wildlife: Tree-climbing lions, flamingos, elephants, zebras, and hippos.

Day 9: Lake Manyara to Arusha – Departure

On the final day of your camping safari, you’ll enjoy a relaxing morning at Lake Manyara before heading back to Arusha. Morning Game Drive: After breakfast, enjoy a final game drive in Lake Manyara, focusing on the park’s birdlife and the large elephant herds. Return to Arusha: After your game drive, head back to Arusha, where you’ll be dropped off at your hotel or Kilimanjaro International Airport for your onward journey.
Meals Included: Breakfast and Lunch
Accommodation: Not included (can be arranged upon request)
Key Activities:
• Final game drive in Lake Manyara
• Return to Arusha
Wildlife: Elephants, giraffes, flamingos, zebras, and more.

Top Activities in Katavi National Park

Game Drives

On a 4x4 adventure across the Serengeti’s endless plains, spotting the Big Five (lions, leopards, elephants, buffalo, rhinos) and witnessing the Great Wildebeest Migration (seasonal).

Hot Air Balloon

Soar above the Serengeti or Tarangire at sunrise, taking in panoramic views of sprawling savannas, grazing herds, and the golden dawn—followed by a champagne breakfast.

Birdwatching

Grab your binoculars to spot over 400 bird species, including flamingos and pelicans, in this lush park known for its alkaline lake and tree-climbing lions.

Cultural Visit

Meet the Maasai people, learn about their traditions, and witness vibrant dances and craftsmanship—an authentic glimpse into Tanzania’s rich heritage.

Walking Safari

Stretch your legs with a guided bush walk, tracking animal footprints, learning about native plants, and experiencing the wilderness up close alongside expert rangers.

Photography Expeditions

Capture Tanzania’s iconic landscapes and wildlife with guided tours designed for shutterbugs, offering prime vantage points and expert tips.
