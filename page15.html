Tour Overview

10-Day Mid-Range Safari Package

This 10-day Midrange safari offers the ultimate Tanzania adventure for discerning travelers, taking you to the country’s most iconic wildlife destinations. Enjoy private game drives, guided by experienced rangers, and stay in Midrange lodges and tented camps. Witness the Great Migration, the Big Five, and breathtaking landscapes, all while indulging in gourmet meals and relaxing in some of Tanzania’s most exclusive accommodations.

Day to Day Trip

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha – Under The Shade

Your journey begins in Arusha, the gateway to Tanzania’s safaris. Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, you will be greeted by your private guide and transferred to the luxurious Arusha Coffee Lodge. Private Transfer to Lodge: After a scenic drive, check in to the lodge, which is nestled within a working coffee plantation. Relaxation & Gourmet Dinner: After settling into your elegant room or suite, you can relax by the pool or enjoy a spa treatment. End the day with a gourmet dinner featuring locally sourced ingredients.
Meals Included: Dinner
Accommodation: Under The shade Lodge
Key Activities:
• Private transfer to Under The Shade
• Spa treatment and gourmet dinner

Day 2: Arusha to Tarangire National Park

On the second day, embark on your first safari adventure in Tarangire National Park, renowned for its vast elephant herds and majestic baobab trees. Morning Departure: After breakfast, you will be transferred in a luxury 4x4 vehicle to Tarangire National Park, a scenic 2.5-hour drive from Arusha. Mid-Morning Game Drive: Start your safari with a private game drive, where you’ll have the chance to see elephants, giraffes, zebras, and more. Tarangire’s unique landscape, dotted with ancient baobabs, creates a stunning backdrop for wildlife photography. Luxury Picnic Lunch: Enjoy a gourmet picnic lunch in the park, set up in a shaded area with sweeping views of the Tarangire plains. Afternoon Game Drive: After lunch, continue your game drive along the Tarangire River, which attracts an abundance of wildlife, including lions, leopards, and a variety of bird species.Arrival at Luxury Lodge: In the evening, check in to your luxury lodge or tented camp, where a candle-lit dinner will be served.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Midrange: Tarangire Katikati Lodge
Key Activities:
• Private game drives in Tarangire National Park
• Gourmet picnic lunch
• Evening at lodge
Wildlife: Elephants, lions, leopards, zebras, giraffes, and wildebeests.

Day 3: Tarangire to Serengeti National Park

On Day 3, you will head to the world-famous Serengeti National Park, known for the Great Migration and incredible wildlife diversity. Morning drive to Serengeti: After breakfast, you have head to Serengeti via Ngorongoro highlands on the way you will get to see the view of the great rift valley. You will stop at Crater view points for some pictures then proceed to Serengeti. Lunch at Mid- range Lodge: Upon arrival in central Serengeti, check in to your lodge or camp and enjoy a gourmet lunch while overlooking the stunning savannah. Afternoon Game Drive in Serengeti: Your first game drive in the Seronera Valley will introduce you to some of the park’s resident wildlife, including lions, leopards, cheetahs, and elephants. The Seronera region is particularly famous for predator sightings. Sundowner at the Lodge: After an exciting afternoon, return to your lodge to enjoy a sundowner cocktail as the sun sets over the Serengeti plains.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Mid-Range: Tanzania Bush Camps or other
Key Activities:
• Game drive to Serengeti National Park
• Afternoon game drive in Seronera Valley
• Sundowner at the lodge
Wildlife: Lions, leopards, cheetahs, elephants, wildebeests, and zebras.

Day 4: Full-Day Safari in Serengeti National Park

Spend the day exploring the Serengeti, witnessing the wildlife-rich plains and the awe-inspiring Great Migration. Sunrise Game Drive: Begin your day with a sunrise game drive, the best time to spot predators on the hunt. The early morning light also provides excellent photography opportunities. Bush Breakfast: Enjoy a luxurious bush breakfast in a secluded spot, with the sounds of the Serengeti’s wildlife as your backdrop. Mid-Morning and Afternoon Game Drives: Continue your exploration of the park, following the migration herds and predators such as lions and cheetahs. Depending on the season, you may witness wildebeests and zebras moving across the Serengeti in search of fresh grazing grounds. Return to Camp: After an exciting day, return to your lodge for a relaxing evening, enjoying dinner under the stars.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Mid-Range: Tanzania Bush Camps
Key Activities:
• Full-day game drives in Serengeti National Park
• Bush breakfast experience
Wildlife: Lions, leopards, cheetahs, elephants, buffaloes, wildebeests, and zebras.

Day 5: Serengeti to Northern Serengeti (Kogatende)

On Day 5, you’ll travel to Northern Serengeti, a region famous for the dramatic Mara River crossings during the Great Migration. Morning Game Drive: After breakfast, embark on a morning game drive in central Serengeti before flying to Northern Serengeti. Flight to Northern Serengeti: Take a short flight to Kogatende, offering panoramic views of the Serengeti’s northern plains. Lunch at Luxury Camp: Upon arrival, check into your luxury camp near the Mara River and enjoy a gourmet lunch. Afternoon Game Drive at Mara River: Spend the afternoon exploring the Mara River, where you might witness wildebeests and zebras crossing the crocodile-filled waters. Romantic Dinner at Camp: After a thrilling day of wildlife viewing, enjoy a private, candle-lit dinner at your luxury camp.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Mid-Range: Baobab Mara Camps
Key Activities:
• Morning game drive in Serengeti
• Flight to Northern Serengeti
• Afternoon game drive at Mara River
Wildlife: Wildebeests, zebras, crocodiles, lions, leopards, and elephants.

Day 6: Full-Day Safari in Northern Serengeti (Mara River)

Spend the entire day in Northern Serengeti, focusing on the Mara River and the thrilling river crossings that define the Great Migration. Morning Game Drive: Begin with a game drive along the Mara River, where you might witness the dramatic river crossings as thousands of wildebeests and zebras brave the waters. Picnic Lunch by the River: Enjoy a picnic lunch by the river, allowing you to stay close to the action without missing any wildlife events. Afternoon Game Drive: Continue exploring the northern Serengeti’s diverse landscapes, which are home to lions, leopards, cheetahs, and large herds of elephants and giraffes. Return to Camp: After a day of adventure, return to your camp for a relaxing evening, complete with a gourmet dinner under the stars.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Mid-Range; Baobab Mara Camps
Key Activities:
• Full-day game drives in Northern Serengeti
• Mara River crossing viewing
Wildlife: Wildebeests, zebras, crocodiles, lions, leopards, and cheetahs.

Day 7: Northern Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater

On Day 7, you will leave the Serengeti and travel to the famous Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the best places to see the Big Five. Morning Game Drive: Before departing, enjoy a final game drive in Northern Serengeti. Flight to Ngorongoro Conservation Area: After your game drive, board a flight to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, where you’ll descend into the crater for an afternoon game drive. Game Drive in Ngorongoro Crater: The Ngorongoro Crater is a unique ecosystem with a high concentration of wildlife. During your game drive, you may spot lions, elephants, buffaloes, and the rare black rhino. Luxury Lodge at Crater Rim: After your crater adventure, check into your luxury lodge located on the crater rim, offering breathtaking views of the caldera.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Mid-Range:Ngorongoro Lions Paw
Key Activities:
• Morning game drive in Serengeti
• Afternoon game drive in Ngorongoro Crater
• Luxury stays at crater rim
Wildlife: Black rhinos, lions, elephants, buffaloes, zebras, and flamingos.

Day 8: Full-Day Safari in Ngorongoro Crater

Spend the day exploring the Ngorongoro Crater, one of the most biodiverse and wildlife-rich regions in the world .Morning Game Drive: Start your day with a descent into the crater for a game drive. Ngorongoro’s rich ecosystem allows for sightings of the Big Five, as well as zebras, wildebeests, and flamingos. Luxury Picnic Lunch: Have a picnic lunch by one of the crater’s hippo pools, offering a scenic break in your wildlife exploration. Afternoon Game Drive: Continue your crater exploration, focusing on its diverse habitats, from grasslands to forests. You may also spot hyenas, jackals, and baboons. Return to Lodge: In the late afternoon, return to your luxury lodge on the crater rim for a final evening in this stunning location. Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Mid-range: Ngorongoro lions paw
Key Activities:
• Full-day game drives in Ngorongoro Crater
• Luxury picnic by the hippo pool
Wildlife: Lions, black rhinos, elephants, buffaloes, zebras, flamingos, and hyenas.

Day 9: Ngorongoro to Lake Manyara National Park

On Day 9, you’ll visit the scenic Lake Manyara National Park, known for its tree-climbing lions and diverse birdlife. Morning Drive to Lake Manyara: After breakfast, drive to Lake Manyara National Park, located along the Great Rift Valley escarpment. Morning Game Drive in Lake Manyara: Enjoy a game drive along the shores of the lake, where you may see elephants, giraffes, and hippos basking in the water. The park is also home to thousands of flamingos and other water birds. Picnic Lunch: Have a picnic lunch in the park while enjoying the beautiful views of the lake and its surrounding landscapes. Afternoon Game Drive: Continue exploring the park, with opportunities to see the famous tree-climbing lions and large elephant herds. Mid-range Lodge at Arusha: In the evening, check into your lodge in Arusha. Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation: Under the Shade Lodge
Key Activities:
• Morning and afternoon game drives in Lake Manyara National Park
• Scenic picnic lunch
Wildlife: Tree-climbing lions, elephants, flamingos, giraffes, zebras, and hippos.

Day 10: Departure from Arushav

Your safari adventure comes to an end on Day 10. After a final morning at Under the shade , you will be transferred back to the airport for your journey back home. Morning Relaxation at Lodge: After breakfast, enjoy a relaxing morning at the lodge before departing for Arusha. Transfer to Kilimanjaro International Airport: After lunch, you will be transferred to Kilimanjaro International Airport for your flight home or onward destination. Meals Included: Breakfast and Lunch
Accommodation: Not included
Key Activities:
• Final relaxation at lodge
• Transfer to Kilimanjaro Airport
