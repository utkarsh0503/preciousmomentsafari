14-Day Ultra Luxury Honeymoon Safari & Zanzibar Beach Package

Tour Overview

This 14-day honeymoon safari blends the thrill of Tanzania’s famous wildlife parks with the tranquility of Zanzibar’s white sandy beaches. Couples will enjoy luxurious accommodations, private game drives, romantic bush dinners, and plenty of time for relaxation and exploration. From witnessing the Big Five in the Ngorongoro Crater to experiencing the Great Migration in the Serengeti, followed by unwinding at a beachfront resort in Zanzibar, this itinerary promises a perfect mix of excitement and romance.

Day to Day Trip

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha – Lake Duluti Arusha

Upon arrival at Lake Duluti Arusha, situated within a lush coffee plantation. Private Transfer to Lodge: Enjoy a seamless transfer with a warm welcome and a bouquet of fresh flowers. Romantic Spa Session: Relax with a couple’s spa session to recover from your journey. Candle-lit Dinner: Enjoy a romantic candle-lit dinner at the lodge’s elegant restaurant.
Meals Included: Dinner
Accommodation: Lake Duluti Lodge (Luxury)
Key Activities:
• Private transfer

Day 2: Arusha to Arusha National Park

Your safari adventure begins with a drive to Arusha National Park, known for presence of black and white colobus monkey and spectacular features like momela lakes, Mount Meru and the Ngurudoto Crater. Morning Departure: Drive from Arusha to Arusha National Park, approximately 1hour. Private Game Drive: Start your safari with a private game drive, capturing stunning images of buffalo, giraffes, and Birds. Luxury Picnic Lunch: Enjoy a gourmet picnic lunch under a majestic baobab tree, complete with champagne. Arrival at Lodge: Arrive at your luxury Lodge for a romantic dinner by a Lake Duluti.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Luxury: Lake Duluti Lodge
Key Activities:
• Game drive in Arusha National Park
• Gourmet picnic lunch
• Romantic dinner by a bonfire
Wildlife: white and black colobus monkey, Giraffe, Baboon, Hippo, hyena, buffalo and a lot of bird

Day 3: Full-Day Safari in Tarangire National Park

Spend the entire day exploring Tarangire, focusing on wildlife viewing, photography, and romantic touches. Sunrise Game Drive: Start the day with a sunrise game drive, ideal for capturing elephants and other wildlife in the soft morning light. Bush Breakfast: Enjoy a bush breakfast in a secluded area of the park. Afternoon Game Drive: Continue exploring Tarangire’s diverse habitats, with sightings of lions, leopards, and various bird species.
• Return to Lodge: Unwind with a bubble bath and champagne in your private suite.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Luxury: Asilia Olivers Camp

Day 4: Tarangire to Ngorongoro Crater

Journey to the spectacular Ngorongoro Crater, one of Africa’s most biodiverse locations and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Scenic Drive to Ngorongoro: Depart for the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, with a stop at a scenic viewpoint for photos. Picnic Lunch on the Crater Rim: Have a picnic lunch on the rim, offering panoramic views of the crater. Afternoon Descent into Crater: Embark on an afternoon game drive in the crater, home to lions, black rhinos, elephants, and flamingos. Arrival at Lodge: Check in to your luxury lodge on the crater rim, where a private dinner with crater views awaits.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Luxury: Ngorongoro Melia lodge
Key Activities:

TRUST IN EXPERIENCE

Top Activities in Katavi National Park

Game Drives

On a 4x4 adventure across the Serengeti’s endless plains, spotting the Big Five (lions, leopards, elephants, buffalo, rhinos) and witnessing the Great Wildebeest Migration (seasonal).

Birdwatching

Grab your binoculars to spot over 400 bird species, including flamingos and pelicans, in this lush park known for its alkaline lake and tree-climbing lions.

Walking Safari

Stretch your legs with a guided bush walk, tracking animal footprints, learning about native plants, and experiencing the wilderness up close alongside expert rangers.

Hippo and Crocodile Encounters

During the dry season, hundreds of hippos cram into small pools—an astonishing spectacle. Nearby, massive crocodiles lie sunbathing in mud tunnels or hunting for prey.
