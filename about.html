About us

Discover Tanzania & Zanzibar Through Unforgettable, Responsible Travel Embark on a transformative journey with Precious Moments Safaris — where every adventure creates memories to last a lifetime. We specialize in delivering authentic, high-quality safari and coastal experiences across Tanzania and Zanzibar, designed to inspire, rejuvenate, and leave a positive impact.


Who We Are

We are a passionate team of travel experts, wildlife lovers, and cultural ambassadors with deep roots in Tanzania.

Our mission is simple: to connect travelers with the rich landscapes, vibrant cultures, and iconic wildlife of East Africa through immersive, personalized, and sustainable travel experiences. From the vast plains of the Serengeti to the lush depths of the Ngorongoro Crater, from summiting Mount Kilimanjaro to swimming in the turquoise waters of Zanzibar and Mafia Island — our curated safaris are designed to match your dreams, your pace, and your travel style.
