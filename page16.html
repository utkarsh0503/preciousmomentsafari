TRUST IN EXPERIENCE

Top Activities in Katavi National Park

Game Drives

On a 4x4 adventure across the Serengeti’s endless plains, spotting the Big Five (lions, leopards, elephants, buffalo, rhinos) and witnessing the Great Wildebeest Migration (seasonal).

Hot Air Balloon

Soar above the Serengeti or Tarangire at sunrise, taking in panoramic views of sprawling savannas, grazing herds, and the golden dawn—followed by a champagne breakfast.

Birdwatching

Grab your binoculars to spot over 400 bird species, including flamingos and pelicans, in this lush park known for its alkaline lake and tree-climbing lions.

Cultural Visit

Meet the Maasai people, learn about their traditions, and witness vibrant dances and craftsmanship—an authentic glimpse into Tanzania’s rich heritage.

Walking Safari

Stretch your legs with a guided bush walk, tracking animal footprints, learning about native plants, and experiencing the wilderness up close alongside expert rangers.

Photography Expeditions

Capture Tanzania’s iconic landscapes and wildlife with guided tours designed for shutterbugs, offering prime vantage points and expert tips.

Day to Day Trip

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha – Serengeti National Park

Your safari adventure begins in Arusha, where you’ll be greeted by your private guide. After a brief introduction to the tour, you will start your adventure to Serengeti National Park, the heart of Tanzania's wildlife Migration. Journey to Serengeti: you will start heading to Serengeti via ngorongoro where you will stop at crater view point for some pictures and then proceed with the journey to ngorongoro. Afternoon Game Drive: After landing, embark on your first game drive through the Seronera Valley, a region known for its high concentration of predators and resident wildlife. Spot lions, giraffes, and elephants as you head towards your luxury lodge. Arrival at Luxury Lodge: In the evening, settle into your luxurious lodge or tented camp, where you can enjoy a sundowner drink while watching the sun dip below the Serengeti horizon.
Meals Included: Lunch and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Luxury: Serengeti Melia or similar
Key Activities:
• Flight from Arusha to Serengeti National Park
• Afternoon game drive in Seronera Valley
Wildlife: Lions, elephants, giraffes, zebras, and wildebeests.

Days 2: Full-Day Game Drive in Central Serengeti

Today, your safari focuses on the Seronera Valley, a prime area for predator action and wildlife viewing, especially during the migration. Morning Game Drive: Start the day with a morning game drive in central Serengeti, known for its excellent predator sightings. Watch lions, cheetahs, and leopards follow the migration herds. Bush Breakfast: Enjoy a luxurious bush breakfast in the wild, surrounded by the beauty of the Serengeti. Mid-Morning and Afternoon Game Drives: Continue exploring the Seronera region. The migration often brings massive herds of wildebeests and zebras through the area, making for spectacular viewing. Your expert guide will take you to strategic spots to maximize your chances of seeing the best wildlife action. Return to Lodge: After a full day of thrilling wildlife encounters, return to your lodge for a gourmet dinner and relaxation.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Luxury:Serengeti Melia or similar

Key Activities:
• Full-day game drives in Seronera Valley
• Bush breakfast in the wilderness
Wildlife: Lions, leopards, cheetahs, wildebeests, zebras, elephants, and buffaloes.

Days 3: Central Serengeti to Northern Serengeti (Kogatende)

Today, you will journey north towards the Kogatende region, famous for the Mara River crossings during the migration season. Scenic Drive to Northern Serengeti: After breakfast, embark on a scenic drive to northern Serengeti, an area known for dramatic river crossings as wildebeests and zebras attempt to cross the Mara River. Lunch at Luxury Camp: Upon arrival in Kogatende, check into your luxury tented camp and enjoy lunch while watching the wildlife roam the area. Afternoon Game Drive along the Mara River: In the afternoon, explore the Mara River area, where you may witness one of nature’s greatest spectacles—wildebeests crossing the crocodile-infested waters. These crossings are perilous but a highlight of the migration season. Evening at Luxury Camp: After a thrilling day, unwind at your camp, enjoying dinner and storytelling around the campfire.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Ultra-Luxury: Sayari Camp by Asilia Africa
Key Activities:
• Scenic drive to Northern Serengeti
• Afternoon game drive along the Mara River
Wildlife: Wildebeests, zebras, crocodiles, lions, elephants, and giraffes.

Day 4: Full-Day Game Drive in Northern Serengeti

Day 4 is dedicated to experiencing the Great Migration in Northern Serengeti, where river crossings and predator-prey interactions are at their peak. Morning Game Drive: Begin with an early morning game drive along the Mara River. The river crossings are one of the most iconic events of the migration, and you may witness thousands of wildebeests and zebras braving the treacherous waters. Picnic Lunch: Enjoy a packed picnic lunch near the river, allowing for uninterrupted wildlife viewing throughout the day. Afternoon Game Drive: Continue exploring the Kogatende region, where predators such as lions, leopards, and cheetahs trail the migration herds. The northern Serengeti’s scenic landscape, with rolling hills and river valleys, offers excellent photo opportunities. Return to Camp: After an action-packed day, return to your luxury camp for dinner and relaxation under the stars. Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner Accommodation Options: • Ultra-Luxury: Sayari Camp by Asilia Africa Key Activities: • Full-day game drives in Northern Serengeti • River crossing viewing at the Mara River Wildlife: Wildebeests, zebras, crocodiles, lions, leopards, cheetahs, and elephants.

Day 5: Northern Serengeti to Central Serengeti

On Day 5, you’ll travel back to central Serengeti for more migration action and predator sightings. Morning Game Drive: Enjoy a final morning game drive in northern Serengeti before departing for central Serengeti. Lunch at Lodge: Have a relaxing lunch at your luxury lodge in central Serengeti before heading out for an afternoon game drive. Afternoon Game Drive: Explore the Seronera region, a hotspot for predator activity, with lions and leopards hunting the migration herds. This area is also known for large elephant herds and buffalo. Return to Lodge: After a rewarding day, return to your luxury lodge for dinner and a peaceful evening.
Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Ultra-Luxury: Serengeti Melia Safari Lodge or similar
Key Activities:
• Morning game drive in Northern Serengeti
• Afternoon game drive in Central Serengeti
Wildlife: Lions, leopards, cheetahs, elephants, wildebeests, and zebras.

Day 6: Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater

On Day 6, you’ll leave the Serengeti behind and head to the famous Ngorongoro Crater, one of the most iconic safari destinations in the world. Morning Game Drive in Serengeti: Before departing, enjoy one last morning game drive in Serengeti to catch any wildlife you may have missed. Scenic Drive to Ngorongoro Crater: After the game drive, you will embark on a scenic drive to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, with spectacular views of the crater rim.Lunch Stop: Enjoy a gourmet picnic lunch en route, overlooking the scenic Ngorongoro highlands. Afternoon Game Drive in Ngorongoro Crater: Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for an afternoon game drive, where you can spot the Big Five—including the rare black rhino—in a unique, concentrated ecosystem. Arrival at Luxury Lodge: After an exciting game drive, check into your lodge on the crater rim, offering panoramic views of the caldera below. Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner
Accommodation Options:
• Ultra-Luxury: ngorongoro MeliaLodge

Key Activities:
• Morning game drive in Serengeti
• Afternoon game drive in Ngorongoro Crater
Wildlife: Black rhinos, lions, elephants, buffaloes, zebras, and wildebeests.

Day 7: Ngorongoro Crater to Arusha – Departure

On the final day of your safari, you’ll enjoy a relaxing morning at Ngorongoro Crater before returning to Arusha for your onward journey. Morning at Leisure: After breakfast, spend a relaxed morning at the lodge, taking in the breathtaking views of the Ngorongoro Crater. Drive Back to Arusha: After lunch, you will drive back to Arusha, where you can be dropped off at your hotel or Kilimanjaro International Airport for your departure flight.
Meals Included: Breakfast and Lunch
Accommodation: Not included (can be arranged upon request)
Key Activities:
• Scenic drive back to Arusha
• Drop-off at hotel or Kilimanjaro Airport
Wildlife: Zebras, elephants, giraffes, and more.
